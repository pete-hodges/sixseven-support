The ultimate "six seven" detection app

What is SixSeven?

SixSeven listens for people saying "six seven" (the viral 67 meme) and alerts you when it's detected. All speech recognition happens on your device — nothing is ever sent to the internet.

Features

  • Real-time "six seven" detection with adjustable sensitivity
  • Fun sound effects and haptic feedback
  • Detection counter, session stats, and rate tracking (67/min, 67/hr)
  • Global community counter — see how many 67s detected worldwide
  • Customizable alert sounds (sound packs available)
  • Share your score with friends
  • Mic modes for normal and noisy environments
  • On-device and server-assisted recognition modes
  • YOLO mode and auto-stop options
  • 100% on-device speech processing — your privacy is protected
