Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

TL;DR: SixSeven uses on-device speech recognition only. We don't collect, store, or transmit any audio data or personal information. Your microphone data never leaves your device.

Overview

SixSeven ("67 Detector") is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app uses Apple's on-device speech recognition to detect when someone says "six seven" — all processing happens locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Audio recordings — We never record, store, or transmit any audio
  • Speech transcriptions — All speech-to-text processing stays on your device
  • Personal information — We don't collect names, emails, or any identifying data
  • Location data — We don't access your location
  • Contacts or photos — We don't access any other device data

Microphone Usage

SixSeven requires microphone access to listen for speech. The microphone is:

  • Only active when you press the Listen button
  • Only active while the app is open and in the foreground
  • Processed entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework
  • Never recorded or transmitted anywhere

You can choose between Normal and Noisy Room microphone modes. You can also select On-Device or Server-Assisted recognition. When using Server-Assisted mode, Apple may process audio on their servers according to Apple's Privacy Policy. On-Device mode keeps all processing local.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Detection count and session statistics
  • Your app preferences and settings (sensitivity, sound selection, mic mode, etc.)
  • In-app purchase records (managed by Apple)

This data is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Global Counter (Firebase)

SixSeven includes an optional global community counter that shows how many "six seven" detections have been made by all users worldwide. This feature uses Google Firebase Firestore to maintain an anonymous aggregate count.

  • Only a numeric detection count is sent — no audio, no personal data, no device identifiers
  • The counter is fully anonymous — we cannot identify individual users or devices
  • Firebase may collect basic analytics data (crash reports, app usage metrics) as described in Firebase's Privacy Policy

Advertising

SixSeven uses Google AdMob to display interstitial ads. AdMob may collect certain device information for ad targeting purposes as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option within the app.

The app uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework and will ask for your permission before any tracking occurs. You can change this at any time in your device's Settings.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Share Feature

SixSeven includes a "Share Score" feature that lets you share your detection stats with friends. Sharing is entirely optional and initiated by you — no data is shared automatically.

Children's Privacy

SixSeven does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app does not require any account creation or personal data to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
